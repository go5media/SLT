About SOXLantern.com

Preserving the legacy of SOX lighting technologies.

Who We Are

SOXLantern.com is dedicated to celebrating and preserving the history of low-pressure sodium (SOX) lighting. From enthusiasts to collectors, we aim to inspire and educate about the beauty and functionality of this iconic technology. Whether you're here to learn, restore, or share your passion, you're in the right place.
